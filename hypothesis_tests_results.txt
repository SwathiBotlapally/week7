HYPOTHESIS TEST RESULTS

1. One-Sample t-Test:

2. Independent t-Test (North vs South):
   Regional sales differ significantly
   Result: p < 0.05 → Significant

3. ANOVA Test:
   Sales vary across regions
   Result: p < 0.05 → Significant
